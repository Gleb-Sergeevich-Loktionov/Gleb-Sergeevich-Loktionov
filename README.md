### 🧠 About me

I'm an **AI specialist and prompt engineer** who turns vague ideas into working products. I break a problem down, define the requirements, pick the right AI tools, engineer the prompts, verify the output — and push it until it actually works in practice.

I work with modern LLMs and AI-assisted development environments — **Claude Code, Cursor, Codex, Replit** — not just to generate text, but as real tools for analyzing code, designing logic, writing documentation, researching markets, prototyping interfaces, and automating repetitive work.

I'm **not a classic ML engineer** — I'm strongest at the intersection of **AI, product, automation, and technical communication.** I'm now moving fully into IT around AI agents and applied AI, and looking for a team to build with.

> 💬 *Strengths: I pick up new domains fast, ask the right questions, structure chaos, take a rough idea to a clear result, work within technical constraints, and explain complex things in plain language.*
